Most comfortable hat EVER!

by Julia Smith | November 17, 2015

I love this hat so much! The color is great for fall and winter, and the material is very sturdy. It is hard to find a good quality hat for the chillier months, but this hat hit the nail right on the head! The wine colored band that goes around the hat is a nice addition and makes it feel very secure on your head. The hat is a tiny bit wide, which is still comfortable, but if you have a small head, this might not be the hat for you. I would definitely recommend this!
